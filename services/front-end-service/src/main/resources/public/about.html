Search the Summits

Search the Summits is a custom search engine built for accessing route beta and mountain trip reports.

Search engines such as Google, Bing and others are exceptionally powerful tools, but they leave you to sift through millions of unrelated results. That's why Search the Summits aims to provide a focused search experience using a curated list of blogs, forums and websites that exclusively create trip reports. We strive to provide a single place to search the thousands of resources on various summits. From the obscure to the obvious. Sites that Google and others cannot index, we are able to; bringing even more information to your finger tips.
