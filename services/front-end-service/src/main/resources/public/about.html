Search the Summits

Search the Summits is a new search engine built for finding trip reports, route information and more.

Search services such as Google, Bing and others are powerful tools, but they leave you to sift through millions of unrelated results. That's why Search the Summits aims to provide a better search experience. Focusing only on websites, forums and journals with trip reports and related summit information. We strive to provide a single place to search the thousands of resources on the world's ranges and summits. From the obscure to the obvious; our goal is to make it searchable.
