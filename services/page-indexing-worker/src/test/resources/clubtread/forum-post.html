Mount Manuel Quimper Dec 1, 2022
Vancouver Island runts When we want to avoid the snow we head to the island. Although everywhere got hit with that recent snowfall, we kept the elevation low enough to avoid it. This was our 6th trip over there this year because island getaway trips are always quite fun. We had an old dog with us so the trips had to be short ones.
Maple Mountain & Stoney Hill Nov 30, 2022 and Mount Manuel Quimper Dec 1, 2022.
Day 1 was Maple Mountain which has tons of mountain bike trails or hiking trails to choose from. We took the Pink trail up and then some random bluff trail down. Would have been better to do the bluff trail up as it's quite steep. Either way it has some good views of the back side of Saltspring Island 4.7km RT and 415m gain. Stoney Hill was just something that an old Dog could do and had a good lookout. 2.55kmRT and 56m gain

Day 2 was Mount Manuel Quimper down near Sooke. Once again this whole area is covered in mountain biking or hiking trails. Lots of different ways to get to the top. Would be a great area to mash out a big km day tagging all the little guys in the area. There is a really nice fire lookout at the top, one of the last ones on the island apparently. 10km RT and 450m gain.
